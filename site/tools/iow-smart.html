IOW / Smart

Manual, Increase-on-Win, and Smart bet-sizing modes for Dice and Limbo — with live stats and a compact HUD overlay.

What it does

  • Manual — bet a fixed base every round
  • IOW (Increase on Win) — ramp up your bet by a configurable % after each win
  • Smart — dynamic sizing with loss-streak reset, wins-before-reset, and min/max base caps
  • Live HUD: wagered, wins/losses, P/L, peak profit, current base bet
  • Auto-stop at a balance threshold to lock in gains
  • Real-time profit chart (up to 10,000 points)

How to use

  1. Install the script for your platform.
  2. Open Dice or Limbo on the target site. The HUD appears in the corner.
  3. Pick a mode — Manual, IOW, or Smart — from the mode selector.
  4. Set your params: base bet, win-increase %, loss-streak reset, wins-before-reset, min/max base, auto-stop balance.
  5. Hit the site's own Autobet button. The script listens to bet results and resizes your next bet per the active mode.
  6. Watch the HUD and chart for live P/L. Auto-stop fires when your balance crosses the threshold.

Mode cheatsheet

Manual

Every bet is your base bet. Useful for measuring variance without any sizing noise.

IOW (Increase on Win)

After each win, the next bet grows by winIncreasePercent. Resets to base on a loss. Classic positive-progression pattern.

Smart

Same as IOW, but also resets when you hit winsBeforeReset consecutive wins, and will reset progression if you hit lossStreakReset losses in a row. Clamped between minBaseBet and maxBaseBet.
