Auto-Vault

Automatically send a percentage of your profits straight to the vault — so you can't lose them back.

What it does

  • Watches your balance and deposits a configurable % of profit into the vault
  • Floating, draggable UI that stays out of the way
  • Per-currency support (BNB, SC, and more) with sensible defaults
  • Rate-limited and retry-aware — won't spam requests or fire during reconnects

How to use

  1. Install the script for your platform above.
  2. Open Stake or Nuts. A small floating Auto-Vault panel appears in the corner.
  3. Set your vault percentage — the default is 20% of profit per trigger. Drag the panel anywhere.
  4. Play normally. Whenever your balance grows past the threshold, the script sends the configured cut to the vault automatically.
  5. Check the log. The panel shows recent deposits and any errors.

Tips

  • Settings are saved to localStorage per site — Stake and Nuts keep separate configs.
  • If the panel doesn't appear, check that Tampermonkey shows the script is enabled on the current domain.
  • Start with a small percentage and raise it once you've confirmed it works on your account.
