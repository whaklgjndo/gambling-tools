Auto-Vault

Auto-vaults a configurable % of your profits so you can't lose them back. Stake + Nuts, with big-win boosts.

Credit to Ruby (of StakeStats) for the Stake autovaulter. The Nuts version is basically a direct port over.

Set your Save % (the cut of profit to send to the vault) and hit Start — the script quietly moves that percentage to the vault at every Check Interval, so you literally can't lose those gains back to the site. Runs headless in a small draggable panel with a live Activity log so you can see exactly what it's doing, when.

Got a spike? The Big-win Threshold (x) defines when a win counts as a big win (anything at or above that multiplier), and the Big-win Multiplier boosts the vault % on those hits — so when you actually stack a big multi, you lock in proportionally more of it instead of letting the base % do the same job for a 100x as a 2x.

Rate-limited to 50 actions/hour (won't get you flagged for spammy requests), idle-aware (won't fire during reconnects), and respects a Min Deposit threshold to avoid dust transfers. Settings are saved per site, so Stake and Nuts keep their own independent configs. Works on stake.com, stake.us, all Stake mirror domains, and Nuts.gg.
